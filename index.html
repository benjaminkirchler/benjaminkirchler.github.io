About

Rigorous evidence
for the energy transition.

I am a PhD candidate at WU Vienna (Vienna University of Economics and Business) and a Research Associate in Energy Economics at the Energieinstitut an der JKU Linz, where I have been working since November 2021.

My research focuses on household energy behaviour, behavioural interventions, energy citizenship, and the socio-economic dimensions of the clean energy transition. I combine field experiments, randomised controlled trials, and empirical methods to generate policy-relevant evidence for a sustainable energy future.

Before joining the Energieinstitut, I worked as a tutor at the Institute of Finance and Public Economics and as a research assistant at the Institute of Marketing and Consumer Research at WU Vienna.
